Who are we?

We have 47+ years of experience in the soap industry

  • 1954

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Safder Amin's Father Late Khawaja Mohammad Amin Kapoor completed his education and started working as a Salesman in Agra, India. After relocating to Pakistan he started trading/supplying soap materials to the Pakistani Soap Industry. With honesty, integrity and dedication he founded Amin Soap & Oil Industries which became a market leader in the soap business in Pakistan.


  • 1983+

    The ascent

    Late Khawaja Mohammad left for his heavenly abode in 1983, after him Khawaja Asghar Amin, Khawaja Salim Ahmed and Khawaja Safder Amin - who were already working under the guidance of their Late father - kept the business progress moving forward to capture the largest market share of laundry soap in Pakistan.

  • 2012+

    RSOC is Born

    After identifying inefficiences in the soap industry, Safder Amin ventured forward to pursue his ideas for a better, innovative and international standard soap business in Pakistan. With his father-like entrepreneurial spirit, armed with a new vision, better team, and a bigger dream RSOC came to life.

  • Looking Forward

    We are a team of creative, technical and hardworking individuals who believe in a commulative responsibility towards a clean and healthy society. To realize our dreams, we are working head over toes to provide affordable soap and equal employment opportunities. We are building a foundation on top of which others can contribute new ideas and build their future. If you believe in telling a story and creating a company that everyone is proud of, than come join us.

Executive Profile

Safder Amin Khawaja

CEO and Mentor

After serving in the Pakistan Army, Safder Amin joined his fathers business. His persistance, honesty and quick decision making helped Amin Group expand to new and rewarding fronts. An example of Safder Amins innovative contribution is the introduction of coconut oil and lemon in laundry soap at a time when no other market player was doing the same. This simple yet unique iteration introduced effective washing properties that catapulted Reshma 222 into success. In 1988 Khawaja Safder Amin was designated to install Soap, Toilet Soap, Detergent Bar, Detergent Powder Plant, Glycerine Plant and Sodium Silicate Plant at Industrial Estate Gadoon Amazai District SWABI KPK. The projects were successfully erected and commisioned. Now, his valuable experience and mentorship is paving the way for RSOC to lead and rethink soap business in Pakistan.
