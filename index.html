  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Safder Amin's Father, Late Muhammad Amin Kapoor, started working as a sales man in New Delhi. After re locating to Pakistan, he setup a shop for trading. With honesty, integrity and dedication he quickly grew the business into Amin Group of Companies that became a market leader in the soap business.
